About Me

I am currently start my third year of PhD in the Rekhi Centre, at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur, India. My supervisor is Prof. Rajlakshmi Guha and joint-supervisor is Prof. Aurobinda Routray. I completed my bachelor’s degree at the School Electronic and Communication Engineering, MAKAUT University , West Bengal. And then I completed my master’s degree in the Electronic and Telecommunication Engineering from Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology (IIEST) Shibpur , India supervised by Prof. Rik Chattopadhyay, in 2020. From 2020 to 2022, I worked as a speech emotion algorithm engineer at Silicon University Bhubaneswar , India.
